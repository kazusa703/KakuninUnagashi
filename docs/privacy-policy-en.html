Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how "Kakunin - Recurring Reminders" (the "App") handles user information. By using the App, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1-1. Information Collected by the App

The App stores all user data (check items, confirmation history, photos, memos, etc.) locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to external servers.

1-2. Information Collected by Third-Party Services

The App uses Google AdMob for ad delivery. AdMob may collect the following information:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA)
  • Device information (model, OS version, etc.)
  • IP address (used for approximate location estimation)
  • Information about ad impressions and clicks

Users who purchase the in-app purchase to remove ads will not have ads displayed, and therefore no such data will be collected.

2. How We Use Information

  • Ad delivery and optimization (Google AdMob)
  • Providing app functionality (managing check items, sending notifications)

3. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS 14.5 and later, the App will request your permission before tracking for advertising purposes. If you decline tracking, all app features remain fully available. Non-personalized ads will be shown instead.

4. Data Storage and Management

  • All user data (check items, history, photos, memos) is stored locally on your device
  • No iCloud synchronization is performed
  • Deleting the App will remove all locally stored data
  • You can export data (JSON format) or delete all data from the Settings screen

5. Sharing with Third Parties

The App does not share personal information with third parties except as required by law. However, information collection by Google AdMob occurs as described above.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Security

User data is stored encrypted on the device with no external access. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as needed. Significant changes will be communicated through the App or on this page.
